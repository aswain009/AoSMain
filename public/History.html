About Aura Of Spring

This section is going to be a more detailed description of the company and where it started.

The idea for Aura of Spring was first started by April Swain, she had the idea to bring the joy of spring scents to everyone all year round. After a few demonstrations and sample offerings, April taught her daughter, Elizabeth, how to brew essences. April realized that she would not have the time to turn her idea into reality with her full-time job. Once Elizabeth got the hang of brewing them, 2 years later she took the idea her mother had started with, and added candles and soaps, and here we are today growing strong, and looking forward to teaching people all about the uses and benefits of essences
